# Empathy Techniques Reference

## Interview Question Bank

### Opening Questions (Build Rapport)
- Tell me about your typical day. Where does [problem domain] fit in?
- How long have you been dealing with [problem area]?
- What matters most to you in this space?

### Experience Questions (Understand Current State)
- Walk me through the last time you [relevant activity]. What happened step by step?
- What tools or methods do you currently use for [activity]?
- What's the most frustrating part of your current approach?
- What works well about how you do this today?

### Pain Point Questions (Dig Deeper)
- When was the last time [problem] really frustrated you? What happened?
- What do you do when [specific pain point] occurs?
- How much time/money/effort do you spend dealing with this?
- What would happen if you just stopped doing [activity]?

### Aspiration Questions (Understand Desired State)
- If you could wave a magic wand and change one thing about [domain], what would it be?
- Have you seen anyone else handle this well? What did they do differently?
- What would "solved" look like for you? How would you know?
- What's the dream scenario vs. what would be "good enough"?

### Surprise Questions (Uncover Hidden Insights)
- What's something about [domain] that most people don't understand?
- Has anything unexpected happened while dealing with this problem?
- Who else is affected by this problem that people don't usually think about?
- If someone built a solution for this, what would make you NOT use it?

### Closing Questions
- Is there anything I should have asked that I didn't?
- What's the one thing you want me to remember from this conversation?

## Assumption Mapping

Before interviews, list assumptions:

| Assumption | Confidence | Evidence | Risk if Wrong |
|-----------|------------|----------|---------------|
| Users want X | Low | None — our guess | High — entire solution depends on this |
| Users currently do Y | Medium | Saw one user do it | Medium — affects our baseline |

After interviews, update:
- Confirmed assumptions — move to "evidence" column
- Challenged assumptions — flag for further research
- New insights — add as new rows
